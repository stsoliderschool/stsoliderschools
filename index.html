FEATURE LIST

The central purpose of the school community is the religious, moral, intellectual, physical and social education of the student. This community seeks to create an atmosphere of genuine care and concern in which the student can grow to maturity. The school is committed to:

Quality Education

What we need is an education system that works for every child, not a select few. This starts with providing a quality education for our youngest so they can learn, grow, and become prosperous citizens.

DISCIPLINED APPROACH

Providing a disciplined atmosphere in which the student is encouraged to grow in freedom and to take increasing responsibility for his own education and for life in the school community.

MORE THAN STUDY

Learning beyond classrooms through extra-curricular activities for all-round development, Ensuring the safety of every child through comprehensive security measures.

OUR FACULTY

At The Saint Soldier Public Sr Sec School, we value our teachers. They, in turn, not only teach the curriculum, but also inculcate an enthusiasm for learning and a questioning, critical disposition.

OUR SCHOOL

The school is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education and follows the curriculum guidelines laid by NCERT from play-way to Senior Secondary Models.The Academic Programme develops thinking and creative skills, promotes rational thought and provides intellectual stimulation. This is the only school in the area where NCC is raised.Value education does not form a separate subject of curriculum but is interowoven into the entire fabric of school curriculum.

450+ STUDENTS

The broad and balanced school curriculum sets the direction, establishes the goals and implements a strategy for getting from where a child is, to where he aspires to be.

30+ TEACHERS

A team of qualified and trained teachers is working with multifaceted potential to help our children to avail multidimensional opportunities without loosing their moral, cultural and ethical moorings.

Extracurricular activities

Numerous opportunities for self expression and team learning are made available to the students through extra curricular activities like Football, Handball, Volleyball, Kabaddi, Kho-Kho etc.
